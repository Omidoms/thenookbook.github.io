Why The Nook Books?

One of the many reasons to pick The Nook Books is because we help support the local community in Eastern North Carolina by finding books that have been discarded from local public libraries, thrift stores, and other donation centers. Furthermore, our books are marked down at a price that can be found anywhere else.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission statement is simply "Books for All". This simple statement shows how much we believe in equal distribution and dispersement of books that have been found in Eastern North Carolina. We believe that every book has a home and homeowner waiting for them.

Business Outlook

As of now, we are currently looking to expand the amount of books that are available for purchase each week on the website. Also, we are looking to expand to include other accessories that go along with our products such as bookmarks or customizable book covers for certain books.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?
As of now, we do not have a current physical location that allows for storefront sales. However, We do have a storage center where the books are kept safely until they are purchased. There is a PO box if you would like to send us anything at: 3400 Wesleyan Blvd, Rocky Mount, NC 27804.
How many books are available?
The only books available are the ones that are listed on the website. The books listed should be rotated each week so that there are options for any other books that a customer may want. As of now, there is a current supply of 500 books that are being rotated through with more being added every month.
Who supplies these books and what are their conditions?
These books are kept in a storage center that are upkept and maintained by staff on location. These books are supplied through many different organizations such as the local churches, public libraries, and other non-profit organizations.
